About The Platform

Built for instant play, modern UX, and consistent player progress.

Hyper Plays is a browser-first arcade hub where users can discover games quickly, launch in fullscreen mode, and keep local or server-backed progress across sessions.

11+

Integrated games with direct launch support

Auth + Guest

Flexible sign-in with offline fallback support

Live HUD

Pause, replay, exit, and highscore tracking in fullscreen mode

Core Features

Secure Access

JWT-based authentication with local fallback for uninterrupted play.

Progress Tracking

Scores and outcomes are saved and reflected in player stats views.

Unified Game Shell

Consistent fullscreen controls and HUD across all embedded games.

Fast Discovery

Search, category filters, and spotlight sections speed up game selection.
